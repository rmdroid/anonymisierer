How the AI Anonymizer Works

What is detected?

The app detects and replaces personally identifiable information in 9 categories:

Person Names
Addresses & ZIP Codes
Email & Phone
SSN & Case Numbers
Dates
Custom Terms

How does the detection work?

Regex Engine Over 20 specialized regular expressions for US/UK government and administrative texts. Detects patterns like ZIP+City, Street+No, SSN, case numbers, and more.
Name Database 200+ common US/UK first names and 150+ last names. Two-word combinations of first and last name are automatically detected as persons.
Context Analysis Honorifics (Mr./Mrs./Ms.), role titles (Case Worker, Judge), signatures (Signed:, By:) and inquiry patterns are used as context triggers.
Local AI Model (planned) Future: Integration with Ollama or LM Studio for context-based NER detection. Fully local, no cloud API.

Custom Rules

Under "Detection Rules" you can toggle each category individually, add custom terms for detection, or intentionally exclude certain terms (e.g. agency names). All settings are stored locally in your browser.

Privacy & Security

Your texts never leave the browser. No data is sent to servers, cloud services, or third parties. All processing happens locally on your device. Settings and custom rules are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — not synced, not exported, not shared.
